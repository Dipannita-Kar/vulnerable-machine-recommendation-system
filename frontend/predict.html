Machine Difficulty Predictor

Uses a trained Random Forest classifier to predict the difficulty level of any VulnHub machine

Note: Difficulty in cybersecurity practice is subjective and may vary depending on a student's prior experience and skill level. CyberPath uses a trained Random Forest classifier to predict difficulty based on objective machine characteristics such as attack path length, estimated completion time, kill chain complexity, and the techniques involved. The prediction reflects the structural complexity of the machine rather than any individual learner's experience.
